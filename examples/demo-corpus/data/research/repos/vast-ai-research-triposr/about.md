# About VAST-AI-Research/TripoSR

The official release of *TripoSR: Fast 3D Object Reconstruction from a Single Image* (Tochilkin et al., VAST AI Research & Stability AI, 2024). See [the paper page](../../papers/arxiv-2403-02151/paper.md) for context.

TripoSR is a feed-forward **Large Reconstruction Model (LRM)** variant: a **transformer** encoder maps a single image to a **triplane** representation that is rendered with **volume rendering** in under a second on a consumer GPU. It is the open-weights answer to the closed **LRM** paper also in the corpus, and is widely used as a comparison baseline by **LGM** and **Instant3D**. Mirrored under MIT — README only.
